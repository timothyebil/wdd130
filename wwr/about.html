About Dry Oar Boating

Our Mission

Dry Oar Boating was founded on the belief that everyone should experience the thrill of white water rafting. Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable river adventures for families and thrill-seekers alike. We are committed to preserving the natural beauty of the rivers we navigate and fostering a love for the outdoors.

Our History

Founded in 1995, Dry Oar Boating started with just two rafts and a passion for the Salmon River. Over the last nearly three decades, we have expanded to cover five major rivers, employing over 50 expert guides while maintaining our commitment to small-group, high-quality experiences.
