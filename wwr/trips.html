The Lazy Serpent

Perfect for families and beginners. This Class I-II excursion offers breathtaking scenery and gentle waves, allowing you to soak in the sun and wildlife without the stress.

For those seeking a thrill, Thunder Canyon features Class III-IV rapids. Navigate through narrow rock walls and experience the heart-pounding "Dragon’s Breath" drop.

Only for the brave. The Gauntlet is a full day of Class V technical maneuvers and massive whitewater. Previous experience is required for this ultimate adrenaline rush.
